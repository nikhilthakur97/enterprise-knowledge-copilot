# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Owner:** Finance, in coordination with People Operations
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Applies to:** All Lumina employees and approved contractors
incurring business expenses on behalf of the company.

This policy describes which expenses Lumina reimburses, the
documentation required, and the process for submitting and
approving claims.

## 1. Eligible expenses

Reimbursable business expenses include, but are not limited to:

- Travel: airfare in economy class for flights under 6 hours and
  premium-economy for flights of 6 hours or longer; train tickets;
  rental cars when reasonable; rideshare and taxis.
- Lodging: standard rooms at business-class hotels.
- Meals during business travel, subject to the per-diem caps below.
- Customer-facing meals where you host external attendees.
- Conference and training fees pre-approved by your manager.
- Software, books, and learning materials charged to the team
  budget.
- Approved home-office expenses (see the Remote Work policy).

## 2. Per-diem caps

Meals are reimbursed up to the following daily limits:

- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $30
- Dinner: $60

Per-diem may be claimed without itemised receipts up to these
amounts. Above the cap requires a receipt and an explanation.

Alcohol is reimbursable only when hosting external attendees and is
capped at one drink per attendee.

## 3. Receipts and documentation

A receipt is required for any single expense of **$25 or more**.
Receipts must show:

- Vendor name and date.
- Itemised description.
- Total amount, including tax and tip.
- Method of payment.

For expenses below $25, a brief description and amount in the
expense system is sufficient.

## 4. How to submit

1. Open an expense report in the Finance portal within **30 days**
   of incurring the expense.
2. Attach receipts and assign each line item to the correct project
   or cost centre code.
3. Submit for manager approval.
4. Approved reports are paid out in the next payroll cycle, usually
   within 10 working days.

Expenses submitted **more than 60 days late** require an exception
approval from your director and may be declined.

## 5. Approval thresholds

- Up to $500 per report: direct manager approval.
- $500 to $5,000: director approval.
- Over $5,000: VP and Finance approval, plus a brief written
  justification.

## 6. Prohibited expenses

The following are not reimbursable, even if incurred during a
business trip:

- Personal entertainment (movies, spa, gym, sightseeing).
- Travel companion costs (spouse, children, friends).
- Traffic violations, parking tickets, or other fines.
- Loss or theft of personal items not covered by Lumina insurance.
- Charitable donations on behalf of the company without prior
  approval from Communications.
- Alcohol-only meals where no business purpose can be documented.

## 7. Currency and exchange rates

Submit receipts in the original currency. The Finance portal applies
the exchange rate on the day the expense was incurred.

## 8. Disputes and corrections

If a reimbursement is denied or paid incorrectly, contact
`finance@lumina.example` within 30 days of the payroll date. After
30 days, corrections may not be possible without a year-end
adjustment.
