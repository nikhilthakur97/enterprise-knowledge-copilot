# Remote and Hybrid Work Policy

**Owner:** People Operations
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Applies to:** All Lumina employees whose role is designated as
remote-eligible or hybrid.

Lumina operates as a **distributed-first** company. Most roles are
remote-eligible, with optional hybrid attendance at our regional
offices.

## 1. Eligibility

- Each role's posting indicates one of three classifications:
  **fully remote**, **hybrid**, or **on-site**.
- Hybrid roles require attendance at the assigned office at least
  **2 days per week** during core business weeks.
- On-site roles require full-time attendance and are not eligible
  for remote work.

Changes between classifications require approval from your manager
and People Operations and may take 30–60 days to take effect.

## 2. Core hours

- Each team defines a 4-hour overlap window between 09:00 and 17:00
  in the team's primary time zone.
- Outside the overlap window, employees may shift their hours as
  needed for personal commitments, provided commitments to teammates
  and customers are met.

## 3. Equipment and home-office stipend

Lumina provides:

- A company laptop and accessories (monitor, keyboard, mouse,
  headset) on request.
- A one-time **home-office stipend of up to $1,000** during the
  first 90 days, for ergonomic furniture or other approved equipment.
  Receipts are required.
- An ongoing **monthly internet allowance of $50** for fully remote
  employees. Hybrid employees are not eligible for the monthly
  allowance.

## 4. Security requirements

Working remotely does not relax security requirements. See the IT
Security and Acceptable Use policy for full detail. Highlights:

- VPN must be used when accessing internal systems from any network
  not owned by Lumina.
- Devices must remain encrypted with the company-managed key.
- Personal devices may not be used for production access.
- Lock your screen when stepping away, even at home.

## 5. Working from a different country

Working from a country other than the one in your employment
contract requires advance approval from People Operations and may be
limited by tax and immigration constraints.

- Short trips (under 30 days per calendar year) generally do not
  require new agreements but must be disclosed before travel.
- Stays longer than 30 days require a written approval from People
  Operations and may require an updated employment agreement.

## 6. Travel to the office

Fully remote employees who are asked to travel to a Lumina office
for in-person work are reimbursed under the Expense Reimbursement
policy. The request must be approved by the manager in advance.

Hybrid employees commuting on their assigned in-office days are not
reimbursed for ordinary commuting expenses.

## 7. Returning to office

If business needs require a remote-eligible role to become
on-site or hybrid, Lumina commits to:

- A minimum of **90 days written notice**.
- Relocation assistance, considered case by case, for affected
  employees who choose to relocate.

## 8. Performance expectations

Performance expectations are the same regardless of work location.
Managers evaluate output, not presence.
