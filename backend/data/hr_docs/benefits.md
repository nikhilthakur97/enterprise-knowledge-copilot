# Benefits Overview

**Owner:** People Operations
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Applies to:** All full-time Lumina employees. Most benefits are
pro-rated for part-time employees working 20 hours or more per week.

This document summarises the benefits available to Lumina
employees. Detailed plan documents are linked in the People
Operations portal and take precedence over this summary if there is
any inconsistency.

## 1. Health insurance

Lumina contributes **90% of the premium** for the standard medical,
dental, and vision plans for the employee, and **75% of the premium**
for enrolled dependents.

- Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your
  start date.
- Open enrolment runs each November for changes effective January 1.
- Qualifying life events (marriage, birth, adoption, loss of other
  coverage) allow off-cycle changes within 30 days of the event.

## 2. Retirement plan

Lumina offers a tax-advantaged retirement plan (401(k) in the United
States or local equivalent elsewhere) with the following match:

- **Dollar-for-dollar match on the first 4% of eligible salary**
  contributed by the employee.
- Vesting is immediate; both employee and employer contributions are
  fully owned by the employee at all times.

You can enroll or change contributions at any time through the
retirement plan portal.

## 3. Wellness and mental health

- **$75 per month wellness reimbursement** for gyms, fitness apps,
  classes, or wellness equipment. Submit through the standard
  expense process.
- Free access to the Employee Assistance Program (EAP), which
  provides up to **8 sessions per topic per year** with licensed
  counsellors, plus financial and legal consultations.
- Two mental-health days per year that count separately from sick
  leave.

## 4. Learning and development

- **$1,500 annual learning budget** for courses, books, and
  conferences relevant to your role. Unused budget does not carry
  over.
- Up to **5 paid days per year** for conferences or training
  programs approved by your manager.
- Internal mentorship is available through People Operations.

## 5. Parental leave (summary)

The full details are in the Leave Policy. In summary:

- 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers.
- 6 weeks fully paid for non-primary caregivers.
- Health insurance continues with no change in employee contribution
  during parental leave.

Lactation rooms are available at every Lumina office. Remote
employees may submit lactation-related home-office expenses against
the standard home-office stipend.

## 6. Commuter and transportation

Hybrid and on-site employees in offices that support it may use
pre-tax commuter benefits up to the limits permitted by local tax
law. Fully remote employees are not eligible for commuter benefits
but receive the monthly internet allowance described in the Remote
Work policy.

## 7. Equity

Most full-time roles include an equity grant subject to a four-year
vesting schedule with a one-year cliff. Specific terms are in your
offer letter and the equity plan document.

## 8. Time off (summary)

The full details are in the Leave Policy. At a glance:

- 20 days annual leave (vacation).
- 10 days sick leave.
- 12 public holidays.
- 5 days bereavement leave for immediate family.
- Up to 12 weeks unpaid leave with approval.

## 9. Life and disability insurance

Lumina provides:

- Basic life insurance equal to **2x annual salary** at no cost to
  the employee.
- Short-term disability covering 60% of base salary for up to 180
  days after a 7-day waiting period.
- Long-term disability covering 60% of base salary, beginning after
  short-term disability ends.

Voluntary supplemental life and disability coverage is available at
employee cost.

## 10. Eligibility on exit

Most benefits end on the last day of the month in which employment
ends. Continuation of coverage may be available under local
regulations (for example, COBRA in the United States). People
Operations sends details with the exit packet.
