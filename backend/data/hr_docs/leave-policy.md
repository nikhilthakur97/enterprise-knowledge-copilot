# Leave Policy

**Owner:** People Operations
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Applies to:** All full-time and part-time employees of Lumina.

This policy describes the categories of paid and unpaid leave available to
Lumina employees, how to request time off, and how leave is approved,
tracked, and paid out.

## 1. Annual leave (vacation)

Full-time employees accrue **20 working days of paid annual leave per
calendar year** (1.67 days per month). Part-time employees accrue
pro-rated leave based on their contracted hours.

- New hires begin accruing on their start date.
- A maximum of **5 unused days may be carried over** into the next
  calendar year. Days beyond 5 are forfeited on December 31.
- Annual leave must be requested at least **2 weeks in advance** for
  any single absence longer than 3 working days.
- The first 90 days of employment are a probationary period during
  which annual leave may be used only with manager approval.

## 2. Sick leave

Employees receive **10 paid sick days per calendar year**, separate
from annual leave. Sick days do not carry over.

- Notify your manager by 9:00 a.m. on the day of absence.
- For absences of **3 or more consecutive days**, a doctor's note is
  required before returning to work.
- Sick leave may be used for the employee's own illness or to care
  for an immediate family member.

## 3. Parental leave

Lumina provides **16 weeks of fully paid parental leave** to the
primary caregiver and **6 weeks of fully paid leave** to the
non-primary caregiver, available within 12 months of birth, adoption,
or legal placement of a child.

- Apply at least 30 days in advance where possible.
- Parental leave may be taken in a single block or split into up to
  two periods, subject to manager approval.
- See the Benefits document for related health-coverage details.

## 4. Bereavement leave

- **5 paid days** for the death of an immediate family member
  (spouse, partner, child, parent, sibling, grandparent).
- **2 paid days** for the death of an extended family member.
- Additional unpaid leave may be requested through your manager.

## 5. Public holidays

Lumina observes 12 public holidays per calendar year, published each
December for the following year. Employees required to work on a
public holiday receive equivalent compensatory time off.

## 6. Unpaid leave

Unpaid leave of up to 12 weeks may be requested for personal,
educational, or medical reasons not covered above. Approval is at
the discretion of your manager and People Operations.

## 7. How to request time off

1. Submit a leave request through the People Operations portal at
   least the minimum notice required for the leave type.
2. Your manager reviews the request and approves or declines within
   3 working days.
3. Approved leave is reflected on the team calendar and your payroll
   record automatically.

## 8. Approval and escalation

If a leave request is declined, you may escalate to your manager's
manager or to People Operations. People Operations is the final
authority on policy interpretation.

## 9. Payout on exit

Unused accrued annual leave is paid out at the employee's final base
salary rate. Sick leave, parental leave, and bereavement leave are
**not** paid out on exit.

## 10. Blackout periods

Certain teams (for example Customer Support during peak season) may
designate short blackout periods during which only emergency leave
is approved. Blackout dates are published at least 60 days in advance
and may not exceed 4 weeks per calendar year.
